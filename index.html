What is MOODÜ?

Snap your photo and MOODÜ will analyze your facial expression, identify your mood and suggest things you can do. Based on your mood and interests MOODÜ will find shopping deals of the day, give you quotes & excerpts to lift you up, give you music playlists, find special events & recreational activities and more. MOODÜ understands you and tries to help!

Find Your Fun

Find fun things to do based on your mood and always be on the up-and-up! With MOODÜ you can know whenever & whereever the "happening" is happening.

Explore New Things

Find new and coming rec places and activities - all based on your interests. In this day and age there's always fun events, sports gatherings and recreational activies. MOODÜ helps you find them!

Be Your Best

Feeling down? Just show MOODÜ and you'll get suggestions that help you be the best you that you can be. Music suggestions, inspiring quotes and excerpts, motivational book suggestions... MOODÜ's got your back!

Any Time, Anywhere

Whip out your phone, open up your laptop, snap a selfie, show MOODÜ how you're feelin' and based on your mood you'll get quick suggestions on what you can do. Find your fun - any time, anywhere.
